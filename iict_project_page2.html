November 20, 2024 Beach Clean Up Drive
▼

On 20th November 2024, the Coastal Community Hub hosted its annual beach cleanup event. It drew over 150 participants from all age groups. Participants collected over 500 pounds of litter, including plastics, fishing gear, and other debris, ensuring a cleaner, safer coastline. Event organisers emphasized the importance of preserving marine ecosystems and expressed gratitude to everyone who contributed.

November 22, 2024 Coastal Market Festival Returns This Weekend
▼

The much-loved Coastal Market Festival is set to return this weekend, promising a day of fun, food, and community spirit. Featuring over 50 local vendors, live music, and kid-friendly activities, the festival will take place at the Harborfront Park on Saturday from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m. Highlights include fresh seafood, handmade crafts, and a special eco-friendly workshop teaching sustainable practices. Admission is free — bring reusable bags for your purchases!

November 21, 2024 Marine Conservation Program Launch
▼

The Coastal Community Hub announced the launch of a new marine conservation program aimed at protecting local marine wildlife and habitats. The initiative includes educational workshops, guided shoreline walks, and citizen science projects to monitor water quality and biodiversity. Community members are encouraged to participate by volunteering for restoration projects or attending informational sessions. The program's first event — a dolphin spotting and conservation talk — will be held this Sunday at Bluewater Bay.
